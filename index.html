These maps show the change in air quality on a weekly basis in London.
Air quality is measured by

  • Oxides of Nitrogen (ug/m3),
  • PM2.5 Particulate (ug/m3),
  • and PM10 Particulate (ug/m3)

Data spans a 5 year period from 08-apr-2016 to 08-apr-2021.
Data was obtained from the London Air Quality Network API.
Lighter colours (yellow, orange) on the map indicate high levels of pollution. Darker colours (blue, purple) indicate lower levels.
